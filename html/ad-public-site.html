Option 2: A Sketchy Website
  1. Go to http://hit-counter-html-code.com/basic-website-counter.html.
  2. Pick the appearance of the tracker and fill out the information.
  3. Generate the code, and copy-paste it into the bottom of the welcome editing page in Super Admin > Edit District Public Fields.
  4. Preview on the public site.
    • The client doesn't need to provide anything.
    • The counter is up to 9 digits.
    • The count starts as you add it in, and you cannot pause it.
