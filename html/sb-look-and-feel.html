Atlas Logo
Requirements for Logo Files from Clients
  • Format: PNG, JPG, PSD, EPS, AI, etc.
  • Minimum Size: 100px by 100px
  • Resolution: 72dpi or higher
Atlas Logo Format and Name Conventions
  • Format: PNG
  • Resolution: 72dpi
  • Naming Convention:
    • school_logo
    • school_logo_small
    • school_logo_notification
Atlas Logo Sizes
  • Size for school_logo:
    • Max-Width: 250 px
    • Max-Height: 170 px
  • Size for school_logo_small:
    • Max-Width: 100 px
    • Max-Height: 70 px
  • Size for school_logo_notification:
    • Max-Width: 36 px
    • Max-Height: 36 px
